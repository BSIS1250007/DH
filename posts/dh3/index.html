Reflection on failures

April 7, 2026

Unit-3 has changed me personally. From childhood I have been hearing that failures are part of life and they make us strong. Sometimes we need to hear them constantly, I need that most. I have committed mistakes during my last exam. I should not have done those things. This chapter not only talks about errors committed by mankind in the field of engineering which resulted in disasters, but teaches how those mistakes have created opputunities to come up better. Commiting mistakes, errors and failures  are essential to be better at something. At the same time being  conscious of where and what we are experimenting with is also important. Another important aspect that I learned in this chapter, which is not part of the unit, is co- ordination among the group and how vital it is. I can’t discuss the case study that I did but really something is there beyond it to share. From my gut feeling, grades are really not at all important in  life. Some units give you knowledge and some questions us. I was already regretting what I did , this chapter psychologically taught me something. I started questioning myself.
